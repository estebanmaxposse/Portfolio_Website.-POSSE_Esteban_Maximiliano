hey folks

I'm Esteban Maximiliano Posse, and I make everything video and sound related turn from good to great. With a little bit of time and creativity, everything is possible.

Since I've started editing on my first year of college, it was always a struggle to get good footage. No one knows how to shoot good-looking takes, nothing about composition, white-balancing, nor sound recording. When I sat on the computer and reviewed all the footage, it was all a mess. And that's when, through a simple Google search, I found about Premiere Pro, After Effects and Audition. I can't say the messy footage turned out great after my first attempts, but I had found my passion those days, making okay stuff look great.
